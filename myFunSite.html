How It Works

We take your request for your item to be "lamped" - submitted through our form which you can access once you sign into your Lamp It! account.You then will be sent a box from us which you will send your item in back to us. From there, we will make it into a trendy lamp and send it back to you within 1 month.

Conditions

Your item must be non-perishable and must be approved through us first. The item can only be sent to us in the container we provide you. If you agree to let us photograph your finished lamp, it may be placed into our gallery on the Lamp It! website.

Mutual Trust

The creation that we make for you is done with mutual trust. In that, if your item is valuable and damaged before we begin the lamp making process, we will reimburse you.

How We Build Your Lamp

This is a system of upcycling / personalizing lighting. We will consider your request in terms of look and do our best to match it. We will contact you if we have further questions.

Mission

We believe in bringing beauty or enhancing the beauty in things. We want to contribute to reducing waste by making beautiful lights with items people want.

This is Lamp It!, an upcycling through lamp making company
